## What is MPLP?

**Multi-Agent Lifecycle Protocol (MPLP)** is an open standard defining the lifecycle, governance, and state semantics for AI agents. It ensures agents are observable, deterministic, and safe.

---

## Governance Structure

MPLP governance operates in **three distinct layers**:

| Layer | Scope | Authority |
|:------|:------|:----------|
| **Protocol** | Specification semantics, normative definitions | MPGC |
| **Documentation** | Explanations, mappings, tutorials | Documentation Maintainers |
| **Website** | Positioning, discovery, reference surface | Website Maintainers |

---

## Standards Alignment

MPLP is designed to **facilitate alignment** with:

| Standard | Relationship | Documentation |
|:---------|:-------------|:--------------|
| ISO/IEC 42001 | Mapping / Enablement | [ISO Mapping](/docs/standards/iso-mapping) |
| NIST AI RMF | Mapping / Enablement | [NIST Mapping](/docs/standards/nist-mapping) |


> [!IMPORTANT]
> MPLP provides **technical primitives** to satisfy process obligations.
> It does not constitute certification or formal compliance.
> Responsibility for regulatory compliance remains with the adopter.
