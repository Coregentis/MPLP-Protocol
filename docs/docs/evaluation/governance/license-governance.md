## 2. What You CAN Do

Under Apache-2.0, you are free to:

- ✅ **Use** MPLP in commercial and non-commercial projects
- ✅ **Modify** schemas, code, and documentation
- ✅ **Distribute** modified or unmodified versions

- ✅ **Include** the Apache-2.0 license and copyright notice
